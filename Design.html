App Concept

I designed an app that would assist in locating hard to find ingredients for recipes. This app allows you to save and upload recipes. It also allows users to make comments and upload pictures of the recipe they uploaded. My design idea for this app was to help users in the kitchen by allowing them to quickly find odd ingredients in local stores.

I hand-drew sketches for some lo-fidelity wireframes to get started with the design process of this app. Drawing out each page really helped me to visualize this app even before it was constructed. After carefully deciding each page layout, I moved to digital sketches using Adobe XD. Then I was able to use the prototyping tool to apply page transitions and functions to my app.
