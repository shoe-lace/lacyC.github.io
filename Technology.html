Java Space Game:

This game has a scrolling background. I edited the space picture so hopefully you can't really tell when the image starts over.

The Objective:

The oxygen option. The bubbles adds to ur oxygen and if the timer reaches 0, game over. If you reach 1 oxygen, ur lives drain. (just thought it looked odd to end game with full life) They do pop when u touch them and popped bubbles won't give you any oxygen. Also, you can't have more then 20 oxygen points at once, no hoarding. (bubbles give you 2 oxygen points)

How to Play:

The player auto shoots bullets and they actually do things. They kill stars. Dead stars can't damage player The stars spin and when they die, they turn into a fireball. The dead star can't hurt you. Player has 7 lives and game ends if you have 0 lives. Healing. The hearts can heal you and u can't add more then 7 lives. They pop too and dead hearts wont heal u.
